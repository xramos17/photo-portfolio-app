Magickal Photography

Magickal photography is a style of photography with fictional scenes and it is probably what i enjoy most doing. They could follow a concept from a book, such as Alice in Wonderland, or create something new from scratch based on simbology or magic traditions .

Landscape Photography

Nature or landscape photography shows spaces within the world, sometimes vast and unending, but other times microscopic. Landscape photographs typically capture the presence of nature but it's not just that. It captures something more and it reaches for some sense of awe.

Concert Photography

Concert photography is the photography of activities relating to concerts and music. It encompasses photographs of a band or musician as well as coverage of a concert. I choose to portray concert photography as ethereal as it can be, choosing to hunt moments and not just watching them.
